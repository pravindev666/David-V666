# 🦅 David Oracle: The Official Options Trading Playbook

This playbook defines the exact rules to follow when trading signals generated by the David Oracle AI system. These rules are strictly defined by quantitative out-of-sample historical backtests (train: 2015–2023, test: 2024–now).

## 1. The Strategy: Credit Spreads 
The system is optimized for **Options Sellers** (Theta Gang). This means we win if the market goes our way, OR if it stays sideways.

- **UP Signal:** Trade a **Bull Put Spread**. (Sell near-the-money PE, Buy 1.5 ATR out-of-the-money PE).
- **DOWN Signal:** Trade a **Bear Call Spread**. (Sell near-the-money CE, Buy 1.5 ATR out-of-the-money CE).
- **SIDEWAYS / CHOP:** NO TRADE. (Do not trade Iron Condors. Volatility makes them likely to fail).

**Hold duration for all trades:** Exactly **5 trading days**.
